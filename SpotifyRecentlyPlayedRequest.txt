GET https://api.spotify.com/v1/me/player/recently-played?limit=50

Headers:
Authorization: Bearer <ACCESS_TOKEN>
Accept: application/json

Body:
empty

Required OAuth scope:
user-read-recently-played

PowerShell curl example:
curl.exe -X GET "https://api.spotify.com/v1/me/player/recently-played?limit=50" `
  -H "Authorization: Bearer <ACCESS_TOKEN>" `
  -H "Accept: application/json"

Notes:
Do not save a real access token in git, chat, screenshots, or logs.
Access tokens are temporary secrets. If you need a fresh one, re-login through the app or inspect it only locally.
